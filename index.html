📄 About This Site

Geometry Mastery is an interactive resource designed to support Year 9–10 students in developing spatial reasoning and geometric problem-solving skills. The website presents key concepts in:

  • ✅ Plane geometry – understanding 2D shapes and measurements
  • ✅ Solid geometry – exploring volume and surface area in 3D
  • ✅ Trigonometry – applying sine, cosine, and angles
  • ✅ Functions – interpreting visual and algebraic patterns

Students are encouraged to explore each module, input their own values, and engage with the formulas through real-time feedback and visual layout. This site was created as a learning tool for classroom and self-paced review.
